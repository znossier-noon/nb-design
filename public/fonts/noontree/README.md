# NoonTree

Custom Figtree build for Noon Business (OFL).

Drop these files here and switch `src/app/layout.tsx` from the Figtree
Google Font stand-in to `next/font/local`:

- `NoonTree-Regular.woff2` (400)
- `NoonTree-Medium.woff2` (500)
- `NoonTree-SemiBold.woff2` (600)

Brand currency glyphs (also in Noon Arabic and Noon Mono):

| Glyph name | Character | Unicode | Currency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `dhm` | UAE Dirham sign | U+20C3 | AED |
| `sar` | Saudi Riyal sign | U+20C1 | SAR |
