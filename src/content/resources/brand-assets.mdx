Everything here is export-ready and pre-approved. If you need an asset that
isn't here, ask the brand team, don't recreate it.

## Logo package

Download the approved package:
[noon Business Logo.zip](/brand-guidelines/noon_Business_Logo.zip).

The package contains the approved Noon Business logo versions:

- **Primary logo**, preferred for website headers, landing pages, presentations,
  email signatures, packaging and trucks
- **Compact logo**, used for app icons, social profiles, small placements and
  mobile-first layouts
- **Full-color logo**, the preferred expression whenever practical
- **Monochrome logos**, only when color reproduction is limited or a single-color
  application is required

Usage rules live on the [logo page](/foundations/logo).

## Imagery

The current guideline examples include landing page, platform, email, packaging,
truck, app icon and social profile applications. These are shown on the
[logo page](/foundations/logo).

## Presentation kit

Slide templates (16:9), document covers and social templates in the brand
fonts and palette, in [downloads](/resources/downloads).

<Callout tone="warning" title="One rule">
  Never recreate brand assets from screenshots or fonts. Drawn letterforms,
  tuned padding and color profiles don't survive recreation, and drift
  compounds.
</Callout>
