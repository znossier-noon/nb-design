## Logo Overview

The Noon Business logo is the primary visual identifier of the brand. It combines
the trust and recognition of the noon wordmark with a dedicated Business identity
designed for professional commerce.

The logo should always be used consistently and without modification.

## Color Variations

## Rules

### Clear Space

Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the brand mark.

### Minimum Size

Primary logo should never appear below `96px` wide in digital surfaces. Use the
Compact logo when space is limited.

### Approved Backgrounds

Use the logo on white, brand blue, background blue, the signature gradient or
quiet photography with enough contrast.

### Incorrect Usage

Do not stretch, recolor, rotate, add effects, place on noisy images or rebuild
the lockup from typed text.
