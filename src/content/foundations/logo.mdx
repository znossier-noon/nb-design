Two lockups: **primary** (lettermark + wordmark) and **compact** (lettermark only).
Pick the variant that keeps the mark legible on your background.

## Variant selector

Move the background, tune hue and lightness, or paste a hex. The recommended
variant updates live, same pattern as [Cash App's logo tool](https://design.cash.app/logo).

## Choosing a lockup

| Scenario | Lockup |
| --- | --- |
| Website header, landing hero, Seller Lab | Primary |
| App icon, favicon, social avatar | Compact |
| Arabic marketing (RTL) | Arabic primary or compact per locale kit |

## Rules

- Tile color is always `#0928FF`, it does not adapt to theme
- Preferred expression: full-color logo on brand or hero blue
- Monochrome only when color reproduction is limited
- Clear space equals the height of the "b" on all sides
