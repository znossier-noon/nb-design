Icons in Noon Business products are functional, not decorative. They compress
meaning into small spaces, and they only work if every icon follows the same
optical rules.

<IconGallery />

## Style

- **Stroke-based**: 1.5px stroke at 16px, scaling proportionally
- **Grid**: drawn on a 16×16 grid with a 1px safety margin
- **Corners**: rounded caps and joins, matching NoonTree's geometry
- **Color**: `currentColor` always, icons inherit text color automatically
- **Fills** are reserved for selected/active states of the same glyph

## Usage rules

<DoDont>
  <Do>
    Pair icons with labels. Icons accelerate recognition of a labeled action,
    they rarely replace the label.
  </Do>
  <Dont>
    Ship icon-only controls for anything domain-specific. A funnel icon means
    nothing until someone teaches you it means "filter".
  </Dont>
</DoDont>

- Icon-only buttons are allowed for universal actions (close, search, settings) and always carry `aria-label`
- Never mix stroke weights or borrow icons from other libraries mid-product
- Directional icons (arrows, chevrons) flip automatically in RTL layouts

## Engineering reference

Icons ship as React components rendering inline SVG with `currentColor`:

```tsx
<SearchIcon className="size-4" aria-hidden />
<button aria-label="Search documentation">
  <SearchIcon className="size-4" />
</button>
```

Decorative icons next to visible labels are always `aria-hidden` so screen
readers hear the label once.
