## Casing

- **Sentence case everywhere**: "Create order", never "Create Order"
- Product names keep their casing: "Noon Business", "Figma"
- ALL CAPS only in Caption overlines

## Punctuation

- No periods on labels, buttons or titles under one sentence
- One exclamation mark per major milestone; zero in errors
- Oxford comma; skip ampersands except established pairs
- No em dashes; use commas, colons or separate sentences instead
- Corner radius tokens are design targets (4px–24px); iOS/Figma squircle smoothing at 100% is applied globally on non-pill surfaces, with automatic radius compensation so corners read the same size as before; `radius-full` stays circular for chips and toggles

## Numbers, money, time

- Numerals always: "3 products"
- Currency: "AED 1,240.50"
- Relative time under 7 days ("2h ago"), absolute beyond
- Durations: "2 min", "3h 20m"

## Localization

- Leave 40% expansion room in layouts
- Never concatenate sentence fragments in code
- Use logical directions ("start", "end") not physical ("left", "right")
