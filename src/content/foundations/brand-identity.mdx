Noon Business is the **B2B marketplace by noon**: wholesale procurement for UAE and
KSA teams. Rooted in noon trust, with a dedicated identity for business commerce.

## Brand architecture

| We inherit from noon | We introduce for B2B |
| --- | --- |
| Trust | Professionalism |
| Recognition | Business-first experience |
| Scale | B2B-focused communication |
| Reliability | Dedicated visual identity |

## The visual idea

Deep confident blue, one cut of yellow, mostly calm with one sharp highlight.
Marketing leads with the [signature gradient](/foundations/colors) and hero blue;
product UI inverts the ratio so blue means interactive.
