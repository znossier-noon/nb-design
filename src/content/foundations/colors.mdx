## Core Brand Colors

The Noon Business color system is built around a distinctive blue palette that
communicates trust, professionalism and confidence while maintaining a clear
connection to the noon brand through yellow accents.

<SignatureGradientShowcase />

presentation covers, marketing banners, packaging and brand showcase assets.
Do not place it inside logos, use it for text or overuse it in product UI.

## Print Guidelines

Use Pantone colors whenever possible for packaging, promotional materials,
signage and other professionally printed assets. Pantone specifications keep the
brand consistent across suppliers and substrates.

## Product usage

Product UI should stay mostly neutral. Blue means interactive. Yellow marks one
recognition moment at a time. Large blue fields belong to brand and marketing
surfaces, not dense workflows.
