## About Noon Business

Noon Business is the B2B marketplace by noon, built to help businesses source,
buy and grow more efficiently.

While rooted in the trust and recognition of the noon brand, Noon Business
introduces a more professional and business-focused identity designed specifically
for wholesale and business commerce.
